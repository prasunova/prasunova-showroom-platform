How it works

Up and running in four steps.

No developers, no integrations — sign up and share your link.

01 / Setup
Create your showroom
Register in under a minute. You get a unique link — showroom.prasunova.com/s/?shop=yourstore
02 / Catalogue
Upload your tile catalogue
Add tile photos from your admin panel. They appear instantly in your showroom — no AI wait time.
03 / Segment
AI reads the room
Our SAM-3 model detects floors and walls in each room photo. Runs once, renders instantly forever after.
04 / Share
Send it to customers
Customers pick a tile, preview it on their own room photo, and download or share the result.
Specification

Built for tile & granite retailers.

Not a generic page-builder — every feature maps to how a tile showroom actually sells.

Real-time canvas rendering
Tile textures apply directly onto the room photo with HTML5 Canvas — no server round-trip, changes are instant.
Customer-uploaded rooms
Customers can upload their own kitchen, bathroom, or living room photo. The AI segments it automatically.
Full catalogue management
Add, price, and categorise tiles from your admin panel — finish, price per sq.ft., and size options included.
Download & share results
Customers download their visualized room as a JPEG or share the link directly — built for WhatsApp sharing.
Isolated per showroom
Every shop owner's catalogue is private to their showroom. Customers only see what you've uploaded.
Zero infrastructure cost
Hosted on GitHub Pages, Cloudflare R2, and GitHub Actions — free tiers cover most showrooms completely.
