Platform Features

Everything a tile showroom needs.

Built specifically for granite, marble, vitrified, ceramic, and anti-skid tile retailers.

🎨
Real-time canvas rendering
Tile textures are applied directly on the room photo using HTML5 Canvas. No server round-trip — changes are instant.
📱
Customer uploads their room
Customers can upload a photo of their own kitchen, bathroom, or living room. AI segments it automatically.
🏷️
Full tile catalog management
Add, price, and categorise your tiles from the admin panel. Show price per sq.ft., finish, and size options.
⬇️
Download & share results
Customers download their visualized room as a JPEG or share the link directly. Great for WhatsApp sharing.
🔒
Isolated per showroom
Every shop owner's tiles are private to their showroom. Customers only see what you've uploaded.
💰
Zero infrastructure cost
Hosted on GitHub Pages + Cloudflare R2 + GitHub Actions. The free tiers cover most showrooms completely.
