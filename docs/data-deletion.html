How to delete your data

Last updated: 17 July 2026 · Version 1.0

  1. Log in at the admin dashboard.
  2. Scroll to Account & Privacy.
  3. Click Delete my account and confirm with your password.

Your account is disabled immediately and permanently erased after a 30-day grace period, including your tile images. Visitor-uploaded room photos are automatically deleted after 30 days regardless.

Request account deletion
